Privacy

This page explains how information is handled on Safety FAQs.

Last updated: 2026-01-01

What We Collect

Safety FAQs is a static reference site and does not require accounts. The site itself does not intentionally collect personal information.

Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard server logs (such as IP address, user agent, and timestamp) to operate and secure the service.

How Information Is Used

Any information collected by the hosting provider is used for security, reliability, and basic analytics. We do not sell personal information.

If you voluntarily provide information through a form or email, it is used only to review and address the issue you reported.

Cookies and Analytics

Safety FAQs does not set its own tracking cookies. If third-party analytics are added in the future, they will be documented here.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted by a third-party provider, which may process standard access logs as part of normal operations.

Data Retention

Any retained information is kept only as long as necessary for operational and security purposes or to address reported issues.

Your Choices

You can browse this site without providing personal information. If you choose to contact us, please avoid submitting sensitive data.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Report an Issue page.

Safety FAQs is a reference-only resource. No legal advice. No substitute for professional judgment.
